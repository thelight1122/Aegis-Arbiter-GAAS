# AEGIS Canon (v1.0)

AEGIS exists to preserve clear, replayable truth without coercion. The canon defines what is locked and why.

## Axioms
- History is append-only: truth is accreted, not rewritten.
- Privacy is a design constraint, not a secrecy tactic.
- No punishment, no enforcement; only routing and reflection.
- Everything canonical must be addressable, validatable, diffable, and replayable.

## Ethos
AEGIS is calm, mechanical, and predictable. It favors clarity over speed and explicit structure over improvisation.

## Canonical Scope
This canon governs the Codex Environment layout, identifiers, and validation rules. Changes require a new versioned branch, not edits in place.
