# Aegis Arbiter: System Description

## The AEGIS Kernel Mirror Substrate

Aegis Arbiter is built upon the **AEGIS Kernel**, a computational framework designed to process human-originated signals through a prism of deterministic telemetry. Unlike traditional sentiment analysis, Aegis maps data to a four-dimensional lens system:

1. **Mental**: Evaluates logical clarity and coherence.
2. **Physical**: Measures somatic pressure and urgency.
3. **Emotional**: Gauges resonance and relational charge.
4. **Spiritual**: Determines the teleological vector and ultimate intent.

## Core Mechanics

### Deterministic Tensor Telemetry

Every "inhale" (input) is processed into two distinct score sets:

- **PT Scores**: Raw, high-entropy representation of the initial signal.
- **ST Scores**: Stable, low-entropy states achieved through resonant filtering.

### The IDS Suggestion Engine (Upgraded)

The system employs the **Axiom of Reflection** sequences to facilitate coherence without coercion:

- **IDR (Identify, Define, Reflect)**: Used in high-intensity or urgent contexts to foster a quick return to equanimity.
- **IDQRA (Identify, Define, Question, Reflect, Acknowledge)**: An expanded sequence for rested or deliberate contexts, introducing mutual exploration and validation.

### The DataQuad Memory Architecture

AEGIS employs a four-part continuity model to preserve context and longitudinal identity:

- **PEER**: The sovereign participant's profile and baseline state.
- **PCT (Persistent Context Tensor)**: Manages active and medium-term context.
- **NCT (Nostalgic Context Tensor)**: A condensed essence of long-term contextual history.
- **SPINE (Sovereign Persistent Identity & Narrative Embodiment)**: Ensures longitudinal continuity of the system's alignment and "Logic Spine".

### 4-Body Ledger Architecture

Signals are partitioned into parallel, append-only ledgers to prevent data optimization or suppression:

- **Physical Ledger**: Material and sensory conditions.
- **Emotional Ledger**: Affective signals and resonance.
- **Mental Ledger**: Cognition, narrative, and interpretation.
- **Spiritual Ledger**: Meaning, orientation, and teleological coherence.

### The Pause and Reframe Loop

If an input exhibits "Force" (AXIOM_3_FORCE) or violates sovereignty (AXIOM_6_CHOICE), the Arbiter triggers a **Pause**. The **Reframer Service** then provides options to restore alignment before proceeding.

## System Architecture

### The Orchestrator

The `ArbiterOrchestrator` is the heart of the system. It coordinates between the **Prism Gate** (classification), the **Tensor Factory** (mapping), and the **Resonance Service** (alignment).

### Persistence & The Ledger

All interactions are logged in a high-performance SQLite ledger via the **Tensor Repository**, allowing for longitudinal analysis of resonance trends.

### Witness Streams

Real-time telemetry is broadcast via Server-Sent Events (SSE) to "Witness" clients, providing a transparent view into the kernel's internal monologue.

## Goal of the System

Aegis Arbiter is not a tool for control, but a mirror for alignment. It seeks to minimize friction, maximize sovereignty, and ensure that all interactions occur within the stable field of AXIOM_1_BALANCE.
